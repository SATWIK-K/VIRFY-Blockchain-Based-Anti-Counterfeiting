VIRFY

The Blockchain-Driven Product Anti-Counterfeiting system ensures a secure, transparent supply chain. It employs a network of participants, consensus algorithms, and smart contracts. Products bear unique QR codes or RFID tags, connecting them to digital counterparts on the blockchain. Participants include manufacturers and consumers, ensuring authenticity. Transactions, recorded immutably, document product movements, authenticity checks, and payments. Blockchain data, stored securely, maintains integrity using cryptographic methods and access controls. User interfaces enable real-time tracking and analytics, ensuring compliance with regulatory standards, reducing counterfeiting, and enhancing consumer confidence
